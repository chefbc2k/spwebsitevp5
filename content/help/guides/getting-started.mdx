# Getting Started with Voice NFTs

Welcome to our platform! This guide will help you get started with creating and trading voice NFTs.

## Connect Your Wallet

1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right
2. Select your preferred wallet provider
3. Follow the wallet connection prompts

## Create Your Profile

After connecting your wallet:

1. Fill in your basic information
2. Add a profile picture
3. Set your preferences

## Record Your First Voice NFT

1. Navigate to the Voice Capture page
2. Click "Start Recording"
3. Record your voice
4. Add metadata and description
5. Set your price
6. Mint your NFT
